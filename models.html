Details on the released models¶

The released MHCflurry predictor consists of an ensemble of models for each supported allele. Each model in the ensemble was trained on a random 90% sample of the data for the allele, and the remaining data was used for early stopping. The predictions are taken to be the geometric mean of the nM binding affinity predictions of the individual models whose predictions fall in the middle 50% of values for a given prediction. The script we run to train these models is in “downloads-generation/models_class1/GENERATE.sh” in the repository.
